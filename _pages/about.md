I'm a physicist-turned-AI researcher and a founding member of the technical staff at [Asari AI](https://asari.ai), where I work on developing AI agents that can help humans co-invent technologies and accelerate the process of research and development. 

I recently earned a PhD in physics from Caltech, working in the [Astrophysics Group](https://labcit.ligo.caltech.edu/groups/astrophysics-group/) of the [Caltech LIGO Lab](https://www.ligo.caltech.edu/). My research focused on using gravitational waves emitted from the collision of black holes and neutron stars to study the physics of these objects. I worked especially on using gravitational wave observations to learn about astrophysics, general relativity, and cosmology, as well as developing the methods involved in these analyses. Within the LIGO Scientific Collaboration, I was actively involved in several working groups, including the Parameter Estimation and Rates and Populations groups.

Check out my research interests and publications for more on my academic background and research.
